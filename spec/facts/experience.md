# Experience

## NiCE - Global IT Support & Services Manager (2023 - March 2026)
Led global Tier 1/Tier 2 support across EMEA, APAC, and the Americas (a team of
roughly 70 people), and drove an automation-first, AI-led transformation of
support and operations.

Designed and deployed multiple production AI agents across different business
units:
- A first-line helpdesk agent that resolves common issues from the knowledge
  base and, when the KB is exhausted, submits a request or incident on the
  user's behalf.
- A sales SKU agent that guides new salespeople to assemble the correct SKU
  suite.
- IT audit agents that run continuous, ongoing audits against compliance and
  security standards (ISO, SOC, GDPR, SOX 1, SOX 2, and others) - automating
  recurring audit work that previously took roughly three full-time roles' worth
  of manual effort, freeing the team for higher-value work while improving audit
  coverage and consistency.

Earlier AI-assisted triage and deflection improved First Contact Resolution
from 65% to 80%. Also owned endpoint management (Intune, SCCM, Jamf, BigFix)
across Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Hands-on macOS experience from A to Z: introduced and onboarded macOS as a
supported platform at NiCE. Before that, MacBooks were not used in the
organization; Shay built out the full macOS support stack - enrollment,
management (Jamf), security, and day-to-day support - end to end.
